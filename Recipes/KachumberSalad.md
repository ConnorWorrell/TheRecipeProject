## Instructions

1. Chop cilantro, peppers, radishes, cucumbers, tomatoes, and onion. Drain excess liquid.
2. Mix with Lemon/Lime juice, Salt, Pepper, and Cayenne.

## Eating Now

1. Serve.

## Prep for Eating Later

1. Store in fridge, add double Lemon/Lime juice.

## Eating Later

1. Serve.
